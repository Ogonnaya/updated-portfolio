OVERVIEW —

From tricky riddles to geography questions, this trivia game has no specific theme. See how you fare against these challenging trivia questions.

In this game, you'll be shown one question at a time. If you answer correctly, you'll move on to the next question. If you choose the wrong answer, you'll be shown the correct answer and then the next question. When all questions have been answered, you'll see how many you got right, and how many you got wrong.
