OVERVIEW —

A random target score is generated at the beginning of the game. Click on the crystals to reach the target score.

The value of each of the four crystals in the game changes every time a new game starts. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Clicking on a crystal will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the target score. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
